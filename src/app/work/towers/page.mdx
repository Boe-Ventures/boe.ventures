## Overview

Living in cities like New York and Oslo, you can't help but look up. The residential towers that define modern skylines aren't just buildings — they're statements about how we want to live. Towers Club was born from that fascination.

## A curated approach

Rather than scraping every building database, Towers Club takes an editorial approach. Each tower is handpicked for architectural significance, design innovation, or cultural impact. The result is a collection that feels more like a gallery than a spreadsheet.

## The collection

The platform features towers organized by city and architect, with rich imagery and key details — floor count, year completed, and architectural style. Users can create accounts to save favorites and build personal collections.

## What we learned

Building on Lovable allowed rapid iteration from concept to live product. The key insight: people care deeply about the buildings they live in, and there was no single destination to explore the best residential architecture globally.
